About

I have been inspired form my long years of experience of watching movies and series to make this little project as a portfolio project for my school. I have always wanted to make something like this where I would have fun and enjoy making with everything I learned so far with ALX and Holberton.

MovieArena is a platform where you can:

  1. follow your friends and see what they are watching.
  2. Rate and comment on movies and series to express your thoughts.
  3. Save your favourite movies and series to watch later.
  4. Add everything you've watched to your profile for everyone to see.
